Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Debt Management?

Debt management is a structured plan to organise everything you owe — credit cards, personal loans, app loans — into one clear repayment strategy, often with revised terms negotiated on your behalf.

How does Debt Consolidation work?

Multiple debts are assessed together and restructured into a single, more manageable plan — replacing scattered EMIs and due dates with one coordinated repayment path.

Will Grow Score negotiate with my lenders?

Yes. Our advisors communicate with your lenders on your behalf to discuss revised terms. Outcomes vary by lender, loan type, and your individual case.

Is my information confidential?

Yes. Every consultation and document you share with us is handled under strict confidentiality, and is never shared without your consent.

How long does the process take?

Most clients receive their financial assessment within 2-3 working days of consultation. The full resolution timeline depends on the number and type of debts involved.
